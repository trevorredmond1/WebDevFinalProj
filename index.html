How many Fender guitar models do you know? Most people might say just the Stratocaster, but did you know Fender offers 6 more electric models, as well as cheaper models of the same design under the Squire line? This website is designed to educate all users to learn about the electric guitar lineup from Fender for 2017! Not only does this site offer an info page, it also allows you to access a Custom Shop page and your very own Guitar Tuner page. To access each page, just click on the buttons for each respective page. To access other pages, click on the Fender logo at the top of the page and it will direct you to the homepage.
